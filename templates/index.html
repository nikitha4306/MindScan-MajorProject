Smart Mental Health Check 💙

A guided check‑in for stress, anxiety, and depression based on the DASS‑21 questionnaire.

This tool helps you reflect on how you have been feeling over the past week.

  • Answer a few simple questions about your recent feelings.
  • Get instant feedback for stress, anxiety, and depression levels.
  • Receive basic guidance on what your scores may mean.
Check‑in info Takes only a few minutes and gives you a clear snapshot of your current stress, anxiety, and depression levels.

This is an anonymous self‑assessment and does not replace professional diagnosis or treatment.

“Checking in with yourself is a sign of strength, not weakness.”
